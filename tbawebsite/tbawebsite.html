Food Bank 2018

Reverend Yvonne Tracey visited The Blyth Academy to collect donations for The Blyth Food bank. Students and staff generously gave food and toiletries which will help provide food parcels in the Blyth Valley Area. Student Ambassadors Ben George, Kara Lunn and Taylor-Leigh Simpson were very proud to hand over the donations from The Blyth Academy.
